KNOCKOUT DANCING




  • Only 6 participants from each group are permitted.
  • Gender neutral.
  • Participants will not be judged on attire,as it is a spot event.
  • Participants will have to report prior to the event to pick up slots and to decide knockout fixtures.
  • Two participants will take stage simultaneosly(based on fixtures) and will compete against each other.
  • The winner of the round will proceed to the next round.
  • Different genres of music will be played.
  • No props allowed
  • Fire,water or any form of powdery substance is not allowed.
  • Any participant found to be violating any rule will be disqualified.
  • Judjes decision will be final and binding.
