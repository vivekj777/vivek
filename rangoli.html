RANGOLI




  • Each group can have maximum up to 2 teams.
  • Each team may not have more than two participants.
  • Rangoli art should be of 2 ft x 2 ft max
  • There is no theme for Rangoli.
  • Maximum time to complete the Rangoli is 90 MINUTES.
  • Teams will be responsible for arrangement of the material required toprepare Rangoli.(ONLY FLOWERS ,GRAINS AND POWDER IS ALLOWED)
  • Participants will have to clean Rangoli after the competition
  • Judging Criteria: 1. Overall Appearance and appeal 2. Details and Clarity in Rangoli art 3. Color combination 4. Creativity.
