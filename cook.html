COOKING WITHOUT FIRE




  • Only TWO teams per group.
  • Each team should comprise of 3 members.
  • Time limit: 90 min
  • Minimum number of items to be prepared: one main dish, one beverage (non alcoholic beverage).
  • No processed food items are allowed.
  • Cutting of fruits and vegetables should be done on the spot.
  • No prior preparations are allowed.
  • Materials required must be brought by the participants.
  • Ingredients and recipe must b ready with the participants at the time of judging.
  • Bread, biscuits and milk products (dairy products) are allowed
