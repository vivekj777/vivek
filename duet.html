Duet Dancing




  • Only TWO teams from each group.
  • Each team should have 2 members (gender neutral).
  • TIME LIMIT: 5 minutes. A warning bell will be rung at 4 minutes indicating 1 minute of time left.
  • 2 marks will be deducted from the total score for every 10 seconds exceeding beyond the above mentioned time.
  • No throwing of powdery substance, water or any other substances including fire presentation is allowed.
  • Animals and vehicles (including bicycles, motorcycles and mopeds) are not allowed.
  • Any team found to be violating any rule will be disqualified.
