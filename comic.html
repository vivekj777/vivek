COMIC STRIP




  • Only 2 teams per group are allowed.
  • 3 members in a team is must.
  • Time allotted: 1 hour.
  • Topic will be given on the spot.
  • Drawing sheet (A3 size) will be provided. All other necessary materials are to be brought by the participants.
  • Only pencils are to be used.
  • Indian ink, crayons, paints, pens are not allowed
  • A minimum of 6 comic grids (3 above & 3 below) is required.
  • Judge decision will be final and binding.
