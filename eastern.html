EASTERN SINGING




  • Only one team from each group can participate in this event.
  • Each group can have a maximum of 12 vocalists and 3 instrumentalists (i.e., maximum number per team is 15 with not more than 12 vocalists & not more than 3 instruments).
  • Vocalists and instrumentalists have to be from the respective groups.
  • TIME LIMIT: 20 minutes (including stage set up time). A warning bell will be rung at 18 minutes indicating 2 minutes of stage time left.
  • Within the allotted time of 20 minutes, the team can perform two solos (one male and one female), one duet (gender neutral) & one group performance (minimum of 5 members).
  • No additional performances besides the above mentioned will be allowed, or the team will be disqualified.
  • 2 marks will be deducted from team total for every 10 second exceeding the time limit.
  • As this is a stage event every team is expected to make use of the given sound system in order to be audible for both judges and audience.
  • Songs in any regional language are permitted.
  • No introduction of singers and/or songs or any other message will be allowed.
  • Audio visuals & karaoke (without vocals) may be used for the solo and duet performances only. The same is not allowed for the group performances.
  • The team should submit the audio visual & karaoke (if required by the team) to the organizers prior to the event. It should be submitted via pendrive only.
  • The organizers will not provide any instruments.
  • Any team found violating any rule will be disqualified.
  • Prizes will be given under the following category: a. Best solo (male) b. Best solo (female) c. Best duet d. Best group
