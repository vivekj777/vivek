MOVIE MAKING




  • Only 1 team is allowed per group.
  • Each team may not have more than 10 participants (excluding cameraman).
  • Maximum time is 8 minutes. 5 marks will be deducted from the total score for each minute exceeding the above mentioned time.
  • There are no limitations placed on the topic, genre or content of the movie. The intention behind the same is to award maximum creative control to the film make.
  • Dialogues, music, imaging is allowed.
  • Editing is not allowed.
  • Only original short videos are allowed.
  • Plagiarism will lead to disqualification.
