MOCK PRESS




  • Two participants per group are allowed to participate.
  • It’s an individual event.
  • Method of play : 1. Each participant will be given a personality a day before the event. 2. The participants are expected to enact the given personality and to answer questions regarding the personality 3. Props and costumes can be used
  • Obscene words and flaring comments are strictly prohibited.
  • Contestants are expected to maintain decorum while they respond to the questions.
  • Time limit: 5 minutes including 2 minutes questions from the judges.
  • The decision of the judge’s will be final and binding.
