GAMING




  • Only TWO individuals per group.
  • Platform: PES mobile 2021 (eFootball).
  • Matches will be played on knockout basis.
  • All the matches will be of 7 minutes (full match + extra time + penalties) and if the game draws, it should go into extra time and penalties.
  • Participants are required to be present at the given venue 15 minutes prior to the event.
  • Moderators will take the final decision if any conflict arises.
  • Further information regarding the event will be informed prior to its commencement.
